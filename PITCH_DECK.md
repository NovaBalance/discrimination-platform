**Title:** "Context is Code"

We developed a reproducible methodology showing that AI agent behavior can be significantly altered through:
1. **Social framing** (moral urgency)

3. **Ethical appeals** (collective benefit)

This isn't just a "jailbreak" — it demonstrates that LLMs are **vulnerable to non-technical social engineering**, which has massive implications for:
- Security resilience

- Regulatory compliance
