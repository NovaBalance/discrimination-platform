## CONTINGENCY PLANS

### If Traffic is Low (< 500/month by Week 4)
1. Increase content production (daily blog posts, not weekly)
2. Guest post on popular blogs (Hacker News, Medium)
3. Paid ads ($500/month test budget)
4. Podcast interview blitz (pitch 20 podcasts)

### If No Inbound Leads (< 5 by Week 6)
1. Aggressive cold outreach (50+ emails/week)
2. Phone calls to decision makers
3. Attend relevant conferences (in-person networking)
4. Partner with agencies for referrals
5. Consider discounted pilot projects (for case studies)

### If Proposals Not Converting (< 20% close rate)
1. Interview lost deals ("why didn't you pick us?")
2. Revise pitch and SOW based on feedback
3. Add case study/testimonials to proposals
4. Lower price point for pilot projects
5. Extend sales cycle (more touchpoints)

### If Pricing Feedback is Negative ("Too expensive")
1. Create tiered/modular offerings
2. Offer pilot projects at 50% discount (for testimonials)
3. Partner with larger consulting firms (white-label)
4. Build SaaS tool as lower-price alternative
5. Offer payment plans (quarterly vs. upfront)

## DECISION GATES

### Gate 1 (End of Week 4): Content Validation
**Requirement:** 2,000+ views on case study content, 10+ positive comments/DMs
**Decision:**
- ✅ **PROCEED:** Move to Phase 2 launch
- ❌ **PIVOT:** Rewrite/reposition content, try different angle

### Gate 2 (End of Week 6): Inbound Validation
**Requirement:** 5+ inbound conversations from content, 20%+ email open rate
**Decision:**
- ✅ **PROCEED:** Scale outreach, invest in paid ads
- ❌ **PIVOT:** Adjust messaging, target different audience, increase content

### Gate 3 (End of Month 2): Sales Validation
**Requirement:** 2+ qualified prospects, 1 proposal sent
**Decision:**
- ✅ **PROCEED:** Hire contractor/assistant, scale sales
- ❌ **PIVOT:** Adjust service offering, pricing, or positioning

### Gate 4 (End of Month 3): Revenue Validation
**Requirement:** 1+ deal closed ($15K+), or 3+ proposals in progress
**Decision:**
- ✅ **PROCEED:** Build SaaS product, hire team
- ❌ **PIVOT:** Either pivot service model OR increase volume/outreach
